WHO AM I???

Hey Everyone, My name is André Henson-Schnee. I was initially born in Belgium but moved to Switzerland at three years old, which started my skiing career. I am a 21 Year Old Student whose favorite is activity is skiing and have probably spent more time on the slopes than in the classroom. Skiing has been an essential part of my family for generations. My great grandfather (his skis looked very different) worked as a ski Patroler and shared his passion with his grandson, my father, who passed it on to me. Most of my skiing has been performed within Switzerland, as I have luckily been to most ski resorts in Switzerland. Now I am hoping to share my love for skiing through this website

The Point of This Website?

Attempting to Ski in Switzerland for the first time, or even for the tenth time, can be overwhelming as we have over 354 ski resorts in Switzerland. Thus, narrowing down which one to go to and finding out about less mainstream gems is what I hope to provide infromation on. I will so provide provide the insider information to help you plan your next ski trip. I information on price, ski area quality, ease of assesibility, night life, etc...

This website will also hope to create a community of skiers who provide photos and reviews on different ski areas users from this website have recently gone to.
